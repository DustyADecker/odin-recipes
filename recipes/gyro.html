Chicken Gyro

Description

This is a Greek-inspired Chicken Gyro. It's fast, easy, cheap, very tasty, wholesome and filling. I can make it very often without getting tired of it. It is a go-to for quick lunches and dinners.

Ingredients

  • Pita Bread
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Vinegar
  • Grilled Chicken
  • Sliced Tomato
  • Sliced Red Onion
  • Crumbled Feta Cheese
  1. Grill chicken and then cut into slices.
  2. While grilling, make tzatziki sauce from greek yogurt, olive oil and vinegar.
  3. Toast Pita bread in an oven or comal
  4. Once bread is toasted, apply a layer of tzatziki on the bread and place the chicken on the tzatziki.
  5. Dress with tomato, red onion, feta cheese and more tzatziki if desired.
  6. Roll the bread into a wrap.
