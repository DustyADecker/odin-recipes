Gyro

Description

The gyro is a Greek wrap often stuffed with lamb, tzatziki, feta cheese, lettuce, onions and tomatoes and wrapped with a pita bread.

Ingredients

  • Pita Bread
  • Lamb Meat
  • Tzatziki Sauce
  • Feta Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook lamb meat. I often pan-fry, but the method I've seen at restaurants is off a rotisserie.
  2. While meat is cooking, prepare tzatziki sauce and chop your desired vegetables.
  3. When meat is almost ready, begin warming the pita bread.
  4. Once meat is finished, place tzatziki first on the pita, then the meat, then the vegetables. Top off with more tzatziki and then some feta cheese.
