Taco Soup

Description

This taco soup is a great recipe for a wholesome and filling dinner. Once you've cooked the meat, it is ready to eat. You can then set it and forget it for as long as it takes for everyone to eat.

Ingredients

  • Tablespoon Butter
  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Greek Yogurt
  • 14oz Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 14oz Can Black Beans
  • 14oz Canned Corn
  • 32oz Beef Broth
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Lime Wedge
  1. In a large pot, bring butter to a sizzle and brown the beef.
  2. In the same pot, add the canned ingredients, broth and seasoning.
  3. Simmer for as long as needed or until desired consistency is achieved.
  4. Once finished simmering, scoop into bowls and garnish with a lime wedge, shredded cheese and a dollop of greek yogurt.
  5. Use tortilla chips for scooping.
