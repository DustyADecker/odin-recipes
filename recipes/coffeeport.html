Coffee Port

Description

The taste of that coffee port from 290 Vinery seems it will always haunt me. I will look for that taste possibly until I take my last breath. Well, maybe this concotion I found on Reddit will make an ok substitute.

Ingredients

  • 1 Part Port Wine
  • 1 Part Decaf Espresso or Decaf Cold Brew Concentrate or 2 Parts Decaf Coffee
  1. In a glass, pour 1 shot of decaf espresso or 1 part decaf cold brew concentrate or 2 parts decaf coffee.
  2. Fill the remainder of the glass with port wine.
  3. Consider adding amaretto; don't know if it would be good, but it was a suggestion on Reddit.
