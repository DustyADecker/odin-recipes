Cheesy Bread

Description

This is a very simple cheesy bread. You could call it bachelor chow.

Ingredients

  • Preferred Bread
  • Cheese
  • Optional Seasonings, such as garlic

Steps

  1. Top bread with shredded cheese, fancy grated cheese, whatever you like.
  2. Add seasonings here with the cheese if you want them, such as garlic.
  3. Toast the bread with the cheese in order to toast the cheese, too. Nice 'n crispy.
